Word guessing Game

Instructions:

  • Each word will be the length of the number of lines on the screen.
  • To guess a letter, enter it on your keyboard.
  • If the letter you select is a letter in the word, the letter will display on the screen in the correct location in the word.
  • If the letter is not contained in the word, then the lines will remain on the screen and the counter will count down.
  • You will have 10 guesses per word.
  • When ready to play, click the Meow button
